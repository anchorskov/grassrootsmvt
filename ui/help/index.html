Field Help

Quick help for volunteers using Grassroots Movement from a cell phone.

Back to hub About Grassroots Movement

Sign in and access

Use the email your organizer approved. If sign-in loops or the page says authentication is required, return to the hub, refresh once, and sign in again.

Start the right workflow

Use Add/Update Voter for a single record, County/City for broad lists, and House/Senate for district work. Start Calls opens phone banking; Start Canvass opens field work.

Tap-to-call basics

Pick a voter card, tap the phone number, complete the call, then return to the browser and choose the result before submitting.

Canvassing on mobile

Confirm your target area, work one address at a time, choose Contacted, Not Home, Refused, or Follow Up, then submit before moving on.

Location/GPS help

Location sharing is optional. Allow GPS only when you want support to see your current field session location, and use Stop sharing when you are done.

Offline or weak signal

If signal drops, stay on the page when possible. Queued or pending work should sync when your connection returns; retry only after the page shows an error.

Pulse consent

Check Pulse opt-in only after the voter clearly agrees to receive texts or email updates. Tell them message/data rates may apply and they can reply STOP at any time.

No voters found or wrong list

Go back to the hub and check county, city, district, party, and voter count. If the list still looks wrong, send the target area to your organizer.

Submit problems

If Submit spins or fails, wait a few seconds, then retry once. Do not repeatedly tap Submit; note the voter ID and message if the problem continues.

Privacy and safety

Do not enter sensitive personal data. Respect opt-outs, No Soliciting signs, and Do Not Contact requests immediately.

Contact support

Send your support lead the page name, voter ID if visible, target city or district, a screenshot if useful, and whether your phone is online or offline.
