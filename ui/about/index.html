About Grassroots Movement

Grassroots Movement is a free voter outreach and volunteer coordination tool created by Skovgard for Senate for use by any Wyoming campaign or civic effort that supports the unrestricted right of every eligible voter to vote and to choose their preferred candidate.

The project helps volunteers organize canvassing, phone banking, voter contact updates, Pulse text-message opt-ins, field support, and campaign follow-up from a phone-friendly web app. It is designed for practical field use: volunteers can select target areas, contact voters, record outcomes, update notes, and coordinate with support teams while working from a cell phone.

Grassroots Movement is built around a simple principle: participation should be easier, clearer, and more accountable. The tool is offered freely to campaigns that respect voter choice and support Wyoming voters' right to make their own decisions at the ballot box.

Current launch priorities

  • Make tap-to-call workflows clearer.
  • Clarify Pulse consent before opt-in is recorded.
  • Improve canvassing support for mobile field use.
  • Strengthen support for weak-signal field conditions.
